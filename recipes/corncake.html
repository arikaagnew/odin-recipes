Corn Cake Recipe

Description

Corn Cake is a quick and easy recipe with only a few ingredients. making it a budget friendly dish. Corn cake is moist, buttery, and so delicious. Corn cake can be a side to most meals and is a favorite with chili in my house. Corn cake is quick which is great for the busiest parent around. Add jalepenos to your corn cake for a little bit of added flavor and heat.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1 can creamed corn
  • 1 can whole kernel corn
  • 1 box corn muffin mix
  • 1/2 tablespoon sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Grease 8 by 8 baking pan
  3. Place the melted butter in a medium bowl and stir in the cans of creamed and whole kernel corn.
  4. Stir in the corn muffin mix and sugar
  5. Cook in an 8 by 8 pan in the preheated oven until the top is slighty browned. About 30-35 minutes
