Instant Pot Mashed Potato Recipe

Description

This is an easy take on mashed potatoes that uses an Instant Pot. The Instant Pot will pressure cook the potatoes while you focus on other things. This recipe is super basic but very tasty and can be updated with your own favorite ingredients. You can also peel the skins off the potatoes if you prefer. This is another super simple recipe for even the busiest of parents.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Yukon Gold potatoes
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 cubes chicken bouillon
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cubed
  • 2 tablespoons milk

Steps

  1. Cut potatoes into 1-inch cubes. Add to Instant Pot with water and bouillon cubes. Select saute and set timer to four minutes on high.
  2. Mix potatoes and water to break up bouillon cubes. Close and lock lid. Select high pressure and set timer ten minutes. Allow ten to fifteen minutes for presssure to build.
  3. Release pressure using natural release method, about ten minutes. Release remaining pressure with quick release method. Remove pot and carefully drain off most of the liquid.
  4. Add sour cream, butter, and milk. Mash with a potato masher to reach desired consistency.
